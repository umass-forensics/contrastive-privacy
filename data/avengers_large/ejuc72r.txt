Does Cap’s character develop, though?

His character has been basically flawless since Day 1. His movies are less about him growing as a person and more about us learning how far Cap will go to defend his principles. 

Cap 1 - He disobeys Army orders and rescues soldiers captured by Hydra. 

Cap 2 - He dismantles SHIELD when he finds that they’ve been compromised by Hydra. 

Cap 3 - His refusal to sign the Sokovia Accords or stop being a hero causes the Avengers to break up. 

Throughout all of that, Cap doesn’t grow. He just becomes more himself. 

EDIT: I’m not saying this is a bad thing, y’all. Cap being this monolith is what I love most about him. Additionally, the Russo’s have expressed this same thing in a recent interview.

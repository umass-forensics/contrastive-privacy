Actors arguing about equal pay is always weird to me. They get paid for their star power, not how much work they put in. For the first Avengers movie Robert Downey Jr got paid 50 million dollars, Chris Evans got paid 2 million dollars. That doesn't mean RDJ put in 25x the amount of work Chris Evans did.

If actors aren't happy with how much they are earning either they need to realise they aren't as much of a star as they thought, or they need a better agent.

I don't think "Avatar"-levels of phenomenon will be guaranteed to happen, even when I do agree that the remake certainly won't pull a "Solo" or end up with grosses closer to "Dumbo." While "The Lion King" is more accessible to general audiences and received a large amount of social media buzz, its overall level of anticipation still trails "Avengers: Endgame" and its predecessor "Infinity War." And speaking of those two "Avengers" films, despite its hype "Infinity War" not only came up well short of "Avatar," but also "Star Wars: The Force Awakens" as well. Even with a likely increase from the 2018 blockbuster's worldwide grosses, I highly doubt "Endgame" will be able to get close to "Avatar" either. In the case of "The Lion King," it may have a shot at surpassing "Endgame" domestically (if the former over-performs while "Endgame" makes less than "Infinity War"), but probably not worldwide.
